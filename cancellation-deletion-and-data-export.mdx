---
title: "Cancellation, Deletion & Data Export"
---

If you need to close your account, shut down a workspace, or export your data, here is how it works.

### **Closing a Workspace**

Individual workspaces can be closed without affecting the rest of your account. If you are an agency managing multiple client workspaces, you can shut down a single client’s workspace while keeping all other workspaces active and unaffected.

When a workspace is closed, all agents within that workspace stop operating immediately. Any phone numbers assigned to that workspace are released. Call history, recordings, transcripts, and configuration data for that workspace become inaccessible. If you need to retain any of that data, export it before closing the workspace.

### **Closing Your Account**

To close your entire account, contact our support team. Account closure is handled through support rather than self-serve to ensure nothing is lost accidentally — particularly for accounts with multiple workspaces, active phone numbers, or running campaigns.

When you request account closure, the support team will confirm the request, process any final outstanding charges for usage incurred up to that point, release all phone numbers associated with the account, and deactivate access. If you have data you need to keep, export it before requesting closure.

### **Data Export**

You own your data. Before closing a workspace or your account, you can export everything:

| **Data Type** | **Export Format** |
| :-- | :-- |
| Call history (metadata, outcomes, timestamps) | CSV, XLS, JSON |
| Call recordings | ZIP archive of audio files |
| Transcripts | CSV, JSON |
| Agent configurations (prompts, settings) | JSON |
| Contact / campaign data | CSV, XLS |
| Post-call extracted data fields | CSV, JSON |

\

Call history can be exported directly from the Call History page in the platform. For bulk exports covering recordings, transcripts, and agent configurations, contact support and we will prepare the export for you.

We recommend exporting your data before requesting any closure. Once a workspace or account is closed, the data associated with it is no longer accessible through the platform.

| ⚠️ GDPR Note: If you operate in the EU or UK, you may have obligations to delete personal data upon request from your contacts. Call recordings and transcripts that contain personal data fall under your data controller responsibilities. You can delete individual call records from Call History at any time, and recording can be disabled entirely at the agent level. See Section 9: Compliance & Security for full guidance. |
