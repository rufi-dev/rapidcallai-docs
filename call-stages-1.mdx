---
title: "Call stages"
---

Every call moves through five stages.

1. Initiated: the platform dials.
2. Connected: the contact answers and the AI begins.
3. In Progress: the agent manages the conversation and executes any triggered actions in real time.
4. Completed: the call ends (by agent, contact, timeout, or no answer); transcript and recording are saved; post-call extraction runs; webhooks fire.
5. Logged: the call appears in Call History with full detail, ready for review and export.
