---
title: "How the Platform Works"
---

RapidCall sits between the world’s leading AI models and the global telephone network.

It is an orchestration layer — the brain that translates your business instructions into real phone conversations.

Every call follows three stages.

1. First, you provide the inputs: a prompt (the script and behaviour), a knowledge base (product and service information the agent can reference), and functions (actions the agent can take during or after the call).
2. Second, when a call connects, the engine processes the caller’s speech in real time, generates a response using your configured AI model, and delivers it back as natural-sounding voice — all within milliseconds.
3. Third, the output: every transcript, recording, extracted data field, and triggered action is logged automatically and visible in your dashboard.

No coding knowledge is required to build and deploy an agent.

The entire platform is configured in plain language.

Technical integrations like CRM connections, webhooks, and API access are available but optional.
