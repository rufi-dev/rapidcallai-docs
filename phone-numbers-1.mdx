---
title: "Phone numbers"
---

Every outbound call needs an originating number, and every inbound agent needs a number to route calls to.

Two options:

1. buy a new number directly through the Rapidcall platform (provisioned via Twilio, available in most countries), or
2. connect an existing business number via SIP trunk (requires E.164 format, termination URI, and optionally authentication credentials).

For US numbers, optional add-ons include Verified Phone Number (\$10/month, reduces spam flagging) and Branded Caller ID (\$0.10/call, shows your business name on the contact’s screen).
