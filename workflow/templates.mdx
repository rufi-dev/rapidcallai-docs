## Available Templates

RapidCall includes four pre-built workflow templates. Load any template from the left panel and customize it for your use case.

## Receptionist

A general-purpose front desk agent that greets callers, identifies their needs, and routes accordingly.

**Flow:**
```
Begin → Greeting → Listen → Booking? ─── Yes → Transfer to booking team
                                    └── No  → AI General Q&A → End Call
```

**Nodes:** 7 | **Edges:** 6

**Best for:** Dental offices, law firms, clinics, hotels, any business that receives general inquiries and appointment requests.

---

## Sales Qualifier

Qualifies inbound leads by assessing interest, budget, and timeline before routing to the sales team.

**Flow:**
```
Begin → Intro → AI Qualify → Hot lead? ─── Hot  → Transfer to sales
                                       └── Cold → End Call (follow up by email)
```

**Nodes:** 6 | **Edges:** 5

**Best for:** SaaS companies, agencies, real estate, insurance — any business that needs to screen leads before connecting to sales reps.

---

## Support Agent

A technical support agent that gathers issue details, looks up the customer's account, and troubleshoots.

**Flow:**
```
Begin → Greeting → Listen (describe issue) → Lookup Account → AI Troubleshoot → End Call
```

**Nodes:** 6 | **Edges:** 5

**Best for:** Help desks, SaaS support, ISPs, any product with technical support needs.

---

## Appointment Booker

A focused booking agent that checks availability and schedules appointments.

**Flow:**
```
Begin → Ask Booking → Listen → Check Calendar → Offer Slots → Book Appointment → End Call
```

**Nodes:** 7 | **Edges:** 6

**Best for:** Medical practices, salons, consultants, tutoring services — any business that books appointments.

---

## Customizing Templates

After loading a template:

1. **Update prompts** — Click each node and customize the text for your business
2. **Change phone numbers** — Update transfer destinations with real numbers
3. **Add/remove nodes** — Drag new nodes from the left panel or delete unnecessary ones
4. **Adjust conditions** — Update keywords to match your specific use case
5. **Connect tools** — Replace placeholder tool names with your actual integrations

## Creating Your Own Templates

Export any workflow as JSON to reuse it:

1. Build your workflow
2. Click **Export** (download icon) in the top bar
3. Save the `.json` file
4. Import it later in any agent with **Import** (upload icon)

<Tip>
Share workflow JSON files with your team to standardize conversation flows across agents.
</Tip>
