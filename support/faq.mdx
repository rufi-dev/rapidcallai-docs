## General

### What is RapidCall AI?

RapidCall AI is a platform for deploying AI-powered phone agents. You create an agent with a prompt, voice, and tools, and it handles inbound and outbound calls automatically.

### Do I need technical knowledge to use it?

No. The dashboard is designed for non-technical users. You write a prompt in plain English, pick a voice, and publish. For advanced use cases (API integration, webhooks, custom workflows), some technical knowledge helps.

### Which languages are supported?

RapidCall supports 30+ languages through multilingual LLM and TTS models. The most common are English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Arabic, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

## Pricing

### How does billing work?

RapidCall uses pay-as-you-go pricing. You are billed for:
- **LLM tokens** — per-token based on the model used
- **TTS minutes** — per second of agent speech (not full call duration)
- **Phone numbers** — $3/month per purchased number
- **Telephony minutes** — per minute for Twilio PSTN calls

There are no monthly fees, no minimum commitments, and no contracts.

### When am I charged?

Stripe automatically charges when your usage reaches $10 (threshold billing), or at the end of the billing period for remaining usage.

### What payment methods are accepted?

Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe.

### Can I see a cost breakdown per call?

Yes. Go to Call History, click on any call, and the Cost Breakdown section shows exactly what was charged: tokens by type, TTS duration, rate applied, and total.

## Voice & Models

### Which LLM models are available?

- OpenAI: GPT-4.1 Mini, GPT-4.1, GPT-4o, GPT-5, GPT-5 Mini, GPT-5.1, GPT-5.2
- Anthropic: Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Claude 4.5 Haiku, Claude 4.5 Sonnet, Claude 4.6 Sonnet
- Google: Gemini 2.0 Flash, Gemini 2.5 Flash, Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite

### Which TTS providers are available?

- **ElevenLabs** — Flash v2.5 (recommended), Turbo v2.5, Multilingual v2, v3
- **Cartesia** — Sonic (fast, low latency)
- **OpenAI** — TTS (good quality, moderate speed)

### What is the difference between Flash and Multilingual voices?

Flash/Turbo models are faster and cheaper (~$0.134/min). Multilingual v2/v3 are higher quality but slower and more expensive (~$0.27/min). For most use cases, Flash v2.5 is recommended.

## Phone Numbers

### Can I use my own phone number?

Yes. You can connect your existing number via SIP trunk. Go to Phone Numbers > Connect Existing and enter your SIP termination URI and credentials.

### How much does a phone number cost?

$3/month per number purchased through RapidCall. Connected SIP numbers are free (you pay your own SIP provider).

### Can I transfer calls to a human?

Yes. Use the transfer_call function in your agent or workflow. The agent will transfer the call to the number you specify.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Call recordings are stored in AWS S3 with encryption. API keys are encrypted in the database.

### Where is data stored?

Primary infrastructure is in AWS EU (Stockholm). Agent servers are in 3 regions: EU (Nuremberg), US (Ashburn), and AP (Singapore).

### Can I delete call recordings?

Yes. You can delete individual calls (including recordings and transcripts) from Call History. Deleted data is permanently removed.

### Is RapidCall GDPR compliant?

RapidCall stores data in the EU by default and supports data deletion requests. Contact support for specific GDPR inquiries.
