---
title: "Post-call data extraction"
---

After each call, RapidCall analyses the transcript and extracts the structured data fields you have defined in advance.

These fields can include items such as:

- call outcome,
- callback time,
- interest level,
- booking status,
- qualification result,
- or any other data point relevant to your workflow.

The extracted data is made available in the **Call Detail Panel**, where your team can review it alongside the transcript, recording, summary, and other call information.

This data can also be pushed automatically to your CRM, database, or reporting tools using webhooks, allowing call outcomes to flow directly into your existing systems without manual entry.
