## Overview

The built-in CRM helps you track contacts, call history, and customer interactions across all your agents.

## Contacts

Each contact record includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Contact name |
| **Phone** | Phone number (linked to call history) |
| **Email** | Email address |
| **Company** | Organization |
| **Tags** | Custom labels for categorization |
| **Notes** | Free-text notes |
| **Call History** | All calls with this contact |

## Call History

Every call is automatically logged with:

- **Agent** that handled the call
- **Duration** and timestamps
- **Recording** with playback
- **Full transcript** 
- **Outcome** (completed, transferred, no answer, etc.)
- **Cost** breakdown
- **Labels** and evaluations

### Filtering Calls

Filter call history by:
- Date range
- Agent
- Phone number
- Duration (min/max)
- Labels
- Outcome

### Exporting

Export call history as CSV for external analysis:
1. Go to **Calls**
2. Apply your filters
3. Click **Export**

## Contact Linking

Contacts are automatically linked to calls by phone number. When a known contact calls, their profile is associated with the call record.
