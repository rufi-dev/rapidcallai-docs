## **On Working With the [RapidCall.ai](http://RapidCall.ai) Team**

Here is something worth being direct about: AI voice agents are not complicated in principle, but they are genuinely difficult to get right in practice.

Getting an agent to pass a test call is straightforward. The prompt needs to be structured correctly. The voice stack needs to match the use case. The call settings need to be tuned for your specific audience and telephony environment. The integrations need to actually fire reliably. And when something is not working, diagnosing whether the issue is the prompt, the LLM, the voice engine, the telephony, or the data requires experience.
