## 1. Create Your Account

Go to [dashboard.rapidcall.ai](https://dashboard.rapidcall.ai/register) and sign up. You can register with email or Google.

After signing in, create a workspace. Workspaces keep your agents, phone numbers, and billing separate.

## 2. Create Your First Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Agents">
    Click **Agents** in the sidebar, then **Create Agent**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the Agent">
    - **Name**: Give your agent a name (e.g., "Reception Bot")
    - **System Prompt**: Describe the agent's personality and instructions
    - **Voice**: Choose from available TTS voices (Cartesia, ElevenLabs)
    - **LLM**: Select your preferred model (GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test with Web Call">
    Click the **Test** button to start a browser-based voice call with your agent. No phone number needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Web calls are free and don't require a phone number. Use them to iterate on your agent before going live.
</Info>

## 3. Add a Payment Method

Before making real phone calls or buying numbers, add a payment method:

1. Go to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. Click **Set up payment** to open the Stripe checkout
3. Add your card details

## 4. Connect a Phone Number

To receive real phone calls:

1. Go to **Phone Numbers** in the sidebar
2. Click **Buy Number** and search by country or area code
3. Purchase a number and assign it to your agent

Your agent is now live and answering phone calls.

## 5. Build a Workflow (Optional)

For multi-step conversation flows:

1. Go to your agent's **Workflow** tab
2. Start with a template (Receptionist, Sales, Support, or Appointment)
3. Customize nodes and connections
4. Publish the workflow
