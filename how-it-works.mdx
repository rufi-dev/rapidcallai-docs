## The Big Picture

RapidCall AI lets you create AI voice agents that answer phone calls, qualify leads, book appointments, and transfer to humans -- all without writing code. Here is how the platform works at a high level.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Agent">
    Configure your agent's personality, voice, LLM model, and knowledge base in the dashboard. Write a system prompt that tells the agent who it is and how to behave.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a Phone Number">
    Buy a phone number or connect your existing SIP trunk. Assign the number to your agent so it starts receiving calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calls Are Handled Automatically">
    When someone calls your number, RapidCall routes the call to your agent. The agent listens, thinks, and responds in real-time using speech recognition, your chosen LLM, and text-to-speech.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Optimize">
    Every call is recorded with a full transcript, cost breakdown, and sentiment analysis. Use analytics and webhooks to monitor performance and feed data into your systems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens During a Call

When a call connects, RapidCall handles the entire conversation loop automatically:

1. **Caller speaks** -- Real-time speech recognition converts voice to text
2. **Agent thinks** -- Your chosen LLM (GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, etc.) generates a response using the system prompt, conversation history, and knowledge base
3. **Agent responds** -- Text-to-speech converts the response back to natural-sounding voice
4. **Tools execute** -- If the agent needs to take an action (book an appointment, transfer the call, look up data), it calls the configured tools automatically

This loop repeats for the entire conversation with typical response times of 800-1500ms.

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | What It Does |
|------------|-------------|
| **Multi-LLM Support** | Choose from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Groq, or custom endpoints per agent |
| **Natural Voices** | ElevenLabs, Cartesia, and OpenAI TTS with dozens of voice options |
| **Knowledge Base** | Upload PDFs and text documents so your agent can answer questions about your business |
| **Call Transfer** | Agent transfers to a human when needed, with a natural transition message |
| **Workflow Builder** | Visual drag-and-drop editor for multi-step conversation flows |
| **A/B Testing** | Test different prompts, voices, and models to find what works best |
| **Webhooks** | Get notified when calls end with full transcript and metadata |
| **Analytics** | Track call volume, duration, cost, sentiment, and custom metrics |

## What You Do Not Need to Worry About

RapidCall handles the infrastructure so you can focus on your agents:

- **Audio routing** -- Calls are automatically routed between phone networks and your agent
- **Scaling** -- Multiple calls are handled concurrently across distributed infrastructure
- **Recordings** -- Every call is recorded and stored automatically
- **Billing** -- Usage is tracked per-call with detailed cost breakdowns
- **Failover** -- If the primary voice provider fails, fallback providers kick in automatically
