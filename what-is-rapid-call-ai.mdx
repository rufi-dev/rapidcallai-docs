---
title: "What is RapidCall AI?"
---

RapidCall AI is the easiest way to launch AI voice agents that make and receive real phone calls for your business.

It is built for companies that want the benefits of voice AI without dealing with complex setup, technical infrastructure, or lengthy development cycles.

With RapidCall, you can either build agents yourself using a simple, easy-to-use platform, or outsource the entire build to the RapidCall team. We help turn your use case into a live, production-ready AI agent without requiring you to manage the technical side yourself.

You define what the agent should do, how it should speak, what it should know, and what actions it should take during or after a call. From there, RapidCall gives you everything needed to launch and run it.

Once deployed, your agent can answer inbound calls, make outbound calls, qualify leads, book appointments, update your CRM, trigger follow-up actions, and log call outcomes automatically.

RapidCall is designed for businesses that want to move fast and keep things simple. Instead of spending time piecing together tools, managing developers, or learning a complex system, you can launch AI phone agents through one platform and one team.

In practical terms, RapidCall gives you two options:

- build it yourself through the easiest-to-use platform in the category,
- or have the RapidCall team build and deploy it for you.

Either way, the result is the same: a real AI phone agent that works around the clock, follows your process, and scales far beyond human capacity.
