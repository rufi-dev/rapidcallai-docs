---
title: "What is RapidCall AI?"
---

RapidCall AI is a platform for building AI employees that make and receive real phone calls. 

You define how the agent speaks, what it knows, and what it does when certain things happen in a conversation. 

From there, it runs on its own — placing and receiving calls, following your script, booking appointments, updating your CRM, and logging everything. 

No manual dialling, no missed follow-ups, no volume ceiling. 

Think of it as hiring a phone rep who works around the clock, never forgets the script, handles hundreds of simultaneous calls, and costs a fraction of a human hire. 

The response latency is sub-second. The integrations are the same ones your human team uses. The difference is scale.
