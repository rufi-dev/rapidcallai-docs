---
title: "Selecting and customising voice"
---

Voice selection is more consequential than most people realise. The wrong voice creates subconscious friction that kills conversion rates before the script even matters.

Match the voice to your audience. A warm, professional female voice works well for healthcare appointment calls. A confident, energetic male voice fits recruitment outreach. A calm, measured tone suits financial services. Listen to multiple voices before deciding, and test your top two or three against real calls before committing.

Speed matters too. Faster pacing suits energetic outreach. Slower pacing works for premium or support contexts. Avoid unnatural pacing — if the speed sounds off to you in a test call, your contacts will notice it immediately.

Test different voices with different LLMs before finalising. The same voice can sound noticeably different depending on which model is driving it.
