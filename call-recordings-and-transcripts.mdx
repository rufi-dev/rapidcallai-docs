---
title: "Call Recordings & Transcripts"
---

Every call generates two things automatically: a full audio recording and a timestamped text transcript. Both are available in the Call Detail Panel — click any row in Call History to open it.

The recording is playable directly in the browser and downloadable as an audio file. The transcript shows each speaker’s lines with timestamps, so you can jump to the exact moment something interesting happened without listening to the entire call.

Recordings are the single most underused feature on the platform. Teams that listen to 5–10 calls a day consistently build better agents than teams that only look at dashboards. The numbers tell you that something is working or not working. The recordings tell you exactly what the agent said, how the contact reacted, and where the conversation broke.
