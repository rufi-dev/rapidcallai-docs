---
title: "Functions"
---

Functions are what allow your agent to do more than just talk.

They give the agent the ability to take real actions during or after a call, which is what turns a conversation into an operational outcome.

Without functions, an agent can answer questions and follow a script. With functions, it can interact with your systems, move the conversation forward, and complete useful work automatically.

Depending on your setup, functions can be used to:

- transfer a call to a human,
- send an SMS,
- update CRM records,
- book a calendar slot,
- check availability,
- press digits in an IVR flow,
- trigger a webhook,
- or pass data into your backend systems.

This is what makes a voice agent useful in production. It does not just speak to the contact - it also performs the actions required to complete the workflow.

In practical terms, functions are what turn the agent from a phone-based chatbot into a working AI employee.
