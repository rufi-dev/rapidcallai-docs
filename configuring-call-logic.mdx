---
title: "Configuring Call Logic"
---

Call logic is the flow: what happens first, what happens next, and what happens when the conversation goes sideways.

Every call has an entry point (inbound pickup or outbound trigger), a flow structure (greeting, qualification, main interaction, closing), conditional logic (interested? proceed — hesitant? handle objection — not relevant? exit gracefully), and actions (schedule, transfer, save data, end call).

Keep flows simple. Anticipate the three or four most common paths and build those out properly. Include fallback responses for anything unexpected. A clean flow with good fallbacks outperforms a complicated flow with perfect coverage every time.
