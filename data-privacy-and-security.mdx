---
title: "Data, Privacy & Security"
---

All call data — recordings, transcripts, extracted information — is stored securely and accessible only to your account. RapidCall does not share your call data or your contacts’ data with third parties.

Recordings and transcripts are stored per call and deletable at any time. Recording can be disabled entirely at the agent level. You control how long records are kept, and records can be manually deleted from your account whenever you choose. Call history can be exported in full, giving you complete ownership and portability of your data. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never stored on our servers. The platform is built on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with session-based authentication scoped to each workspace.

| 📋 GDPR Note: If you operate in the EU or UK, it is your responsibility to ensure your use of the platform complies with applicable data protection regulations. This includes ensuring a lawful basis for processing personal data and, where required, informing callers that the call may be recorded or handled by AI. See Section 9: Compliance & Security for detailed guidance. |
