title: "Custom Dashboards"
---

The built-in Performance dashboard covers what most teams need day to day. But every business tracks success differently, and the standard metrics may not capture everything that matters to you.

If you need additional reporting views — campaign-specific conversion funnels, agent comparison matrices, cost-per-outcome breakdowns, territory-level performance splits, or anything else specific to how your operation runs — reach out to the RapidCall team. We build custom dashboards on request, tailored to the KPIs and reporting structure your business actually uses.

This is particularly relevant for agencies managing multiple client workspaces, enterprise teams reporting to senior leadership, and businesses running high-volume campaigns where standard metrics need to be sliced by segment, geography, or time window.

| 📬 To request a custom dashboard: contact the team via the Help button in the platform, email [hello@rapidcall.ai](mailto:hello@rapidcall.ai), or raise it during your next review call. We will scope what you need and build it. |
